How Learning AI?

Google Artificial Intelligence Course

Google is one of the top players in AI/machine learning, and they offer their artificial intelligence crash course for free—the same one they require all of their engineers to take.

The whole course is about 15 hours long and we highly recommend it for any marketer.

HubSpot Academy's Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning for Marketing

This course from HubSpot Academy offers an intro to AI and machine learning and teaches you how to distinguish each one. It also teaches you how to apply them in your marketing. Oh, and it'll show you how to start piloting AI.

Stanford Artificial Intelligence Course

There’s a free online artificial intelligence course on Coursera, offered through Stanford University and taught by Andrew Ng. It’s titled “machine learning” and is 100% free to take—you’ll only need to pay if you want a certification. It’s a little more intensive than Google’s artificial intelligence course with 11 weeks of learning comprised of 5-8 readings and videos each week.

HUdacity Artificial Intelligence Course

they’ve loaded up that same free artificial intelligence course on Udacity, where marketers can learn the basics of artificial intelligence and its growing applications in business.

It has an estimated timeline of four months, is 100% free, and counts as credit towards their machine learning and data analyst nano degree programs for those who choose to pursue them.
